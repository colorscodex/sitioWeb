COLORSCODEX

Presents a Vegan cuisine with Mediterranean Savor to introduce or help to complete, nutritionally, other types of diet that may be lacking in fruits and vegetables. They are simple and easy ideas. As well as, many other novelties.

Intro with Video

Colorscodex is a photographic guide about dishes made only with vegetables, where many of the recipes come through ancient farmers from the regions of Castilla and León and Castilla - La Mancha in Spain; Northern Portugal and Provence in France, among other places.

Other recipes come through my family, made as before, with calm and thinking of the well-being of the same. They are, as the Pumpkin cream soup recipes that always bring back good memories, memories as my grandmother did.
